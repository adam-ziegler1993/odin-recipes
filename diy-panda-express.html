DIY Panda Express Orange Chicken

A mouth-watering DIY Panda Express Orange Chicken

Enjoy the sweet and tangy flavors of Panda Express Orange Chicken right in your own kitchen with this easy recipe.

A serving of Orange Chicken at Panda Express costs around $7. You can make this at home for about $4 per serving!

Here's what you'll need to make 4 servings:

  • 1lb Boneless, skinless chicken breast
  • 1 cup cornstarch
  • 1 egg
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup white vinegar
  • 1/4 cup orange juice
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1 tablespoon ginger, minced
  • 1 tablespoon garlic, minced
  • 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 2 green onions, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch (for sauce)
  • 1/4 cup water
  • Oil for frying

Let's put it together:

  1. Cut the chicken breast into bite-sized pieces and set aside.
  2. In a bowl, whisk together the egg and 1/4 cup cornstarch. Add the chicken pieces and coat them well.
  3. Heat oil in a deep fryer or large skillet over medium-high heat. Fry the chicken pieces until golden brown and crispy, about 3-4 minutes per side. Remove and drain on paper towels.
  4. In a separate pan, combine soy sauce, white vinegar, orange juice, sugar, ginger, garlic, and red pepper flakes. Bring to a simmer.
  5. In a small bowl, mix 1 tablespoon cornstarch with 1/4 cup water. Add this mixture to the pan and stir until the sauce thickens.
  6. Add the fried chicken pieces to the sauce and toss to coat evenly.
  7. Garnish with chopped green onions and serve immediately with steamed rice.
