DIY Chipotle Bowl

A lovely & loaded Chipotle bowl model

You'll fall in love at first bite with this diy Chipotle Bowl. It's so simple and easy to make, but the whole family will love it and so will your wallet.

One bowl at Chipotle costs around $12. You can make this for about $7 and save $5/meal!

Here's what you'll need to make 4 bowls:

  • 1lb Grass-fed, Grass-finished beef
  • 2 cups of dry rice
  • 6 normal tomatoes
  • 1 onion
  • 2 avocados
  • 1lb frozen corn
  • 2 cups shredded cheese
  • 1 bag of spinach
  • 1 tub of sour cream
  • 2 bags of chips

Let's put it together:

  1. At the same time, add the 1lb ground beef to a pan set on high and 2 cups of rice to a pot of water. Bring the rice pot to a boil and let it sit, stirring occasionally.
  2. While the beef and rice are cooking, chop the tomatoes and onions up and place into a bowl.
  3. Cut the avocado in half, take out the seed, and slice the avocado into cubes... Be sure to keep the peel on so you can set it on the counter
  4. Grab a handful of spinach and place in each bowl. Do 1 cut vertically and 1 cut horizontally through the spinach leaves.
  5. By now, the beef should be done. Set the beef in a separate container.
  6. We want to use the fat from the ground beef to cook the corn. So place the frozen corn in the beef pan and place on high until thawed.
  7. Starting to prepar the bowls, you'll start by putting a 1/2 cup of rice on top of the spinach
  8. Then, the ground beef. However much is left after cooking, split into 4 equal amounts and place on top of the rice.
  9. The tomatoes and onions go on top next.
  10. Cut the cubes of avocado out of the shell and place on the bowls.
  11. Sprinkle some cheese on top, with a dollop of sour cream.
